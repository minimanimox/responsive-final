Help & Contact Us

Do you have comments or questions? Just want to say hello? We’d love to hear from you. Fill out the form below, and we’ll connect with you soon.

If you forgot your password, sign in and click 'forgot password?' Enter the email address associated with your account, then click 'send.' We will send you a link via email to reset your password. Please allow a few minutes for the request. You will have 24 hours to reset your password using the link provided in the email. If your link has expired, request a new link by following the same steps above.

If you were unable to reset your password or you didn't receive an email notification, please contact us via e-mail or phone at (604) 899-4480.

Once an order is processing, we are unable to change or cancel it. Our system is designed to process and ship orders. For any questions, please contact Customer Service online or call 888-839-9638.

In rare instances, we may need to cancel all or part of an order for various reasons, including:
  • Unavailable product(s)
  • Difficulty in processing your payment information
  • Inability to ship to address provided
  • Existence of a duplicate order
If your order is cancelled, you will receive an e-mail notification. You will not be billed for any cancelled products. Once an order is cancelled, it cannot be reprocessed and must be submitted again on our website.

When adding items to your cart, choose ‘Ship to Me' by clicking on ‘check availability in my area’. You will be prompted to enter the postal code where your items will ship to. Standard Shipping is available across all Canadian provinces except Quebec. Special order items or shipments to rural addresses may exceed the standard shipping delivery window and may not be available for Ship to Home. Shipments are not available to post office box addresses.

Live pets, canned, fresh or frozen foods, select cat litters, aquariums and other select products may not be eligible for shipping. Shipping costs are based on the products ordered. Due to size and/or weight, certain items bear a shipping surcharge or special handling fee, which will still apply. Total shipping charges are automatically calculated during Checkout prior to completing your order. If your order qualifies for free shipping($49 and above before taxes, with Treats™ membership), your discount will appear in the Order Summary.
